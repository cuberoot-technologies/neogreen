Neo Green Trade LLP is a dynamic and progressive enterprise in the international commodities trading market, with a focus on sustainability and innovation. Driven by cutting-edge technology, we bring together the best of sustainability and innovation in the world of international trade. Our core operations encompass trading in carbon credits, green energy solutions, and solar and hydrogen technologies, as well as the distribution of solar cook stoves. We are also engaged in the marketing of a wide range of goods, the import and export of diverse agro products, minerals, fuel trade, and IT services.

Our Vision

We aim to lead the global transition towards a sustainable and green economy by promoting eco-friendly commodities and innovative technologies. We envision a world driven by sustainable growth. At Neo Green Trade LLP, we aim to emerge as a lead player in international green trade while fostering growth for our partners, customers, and communities.
